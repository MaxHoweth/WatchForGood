About Our App

Watch For Good is a charitable social network and marketplace. Our goal is to increase the corporate social responsibility footprint within the giving community through sponsorships, fundraising, advocacy, and commerce.

Cause Based Platform

We offer a cause based platform that allows brands and nonprofit organizations to share their political, social, and enviromental values with our audience through video.

Impression based Crowdfunding

We in turn monetize the content and share a portion of the advertising revenue with the nonprofits every time a video is viewed.

Tokens as Rewards

Our network is powered by a micro-economy which uses tokens as rewards for watching cause based videos.

Purchase Pledges

When subscribers/users accumulate enough tokens they can use their tokens to purchase pledges donated by brand sponsors.

Purchase Ad Space

Introduce your Product

How Our App Works

Watch for Good is a fundraising social network and marketplace. What separates us from other crowdfunding platforms is our hybrid network that allows users to contribute financially by simply watching videos that include advertising. The network requires brands and nonprofits to create their own cause based video campaigns which are monetized through online advertising. We in turn share a portion of the proceeds with the nonprofit organizations

Our network is powered by a micro economy which uses tokens as rewards for watching cause based videos. When users accumulate enough tokens, they can then use their tokens to purchase pledges donated by brand sponsors. We partner with corporate sponsors interested in putting their social impact dollars to work by selling them advertising space and an e-commerce marketplace in exchange for pledges in the form of in-kind donations. Once brands have fulfilled their donations requirements they can introduce their product into the marketplace.

Frequently Asked Questions

+Are there any fees for users or non-profits?

No, our service is completley free for users and non-profits.

+Can nonprofits from outside of North America sign up for the network?

Yes, our platform is available to all nonprofits worldwide.

+What kind of product donations will brands be making to the marketplace?

The type of donations is solely up to the sponsor.

+Who pays for the shipping of donated items to users?

Brand's sponsors will be responsible for shipping donations to users.

+What is a pledge?

A pledge is a gift/donation given by corporate sponsors in support of the contribution made by the user.

+How much revenue will be shared with the nonprofits?

Nonprofits will recieve 40% of the cost per impression, this is the revenue generated from the monetized videos.

+Can all items in the marketplace be purchased with tokens?

No, only the items earmarked as donations can be purchased with tokens.

+Can users post videos?

No, only nonprofits and brands can post videos.

+Can brands use their own ads?

Yes, through our sponsorship packages brands can create their own ads.

+When brand sponsors create videos where does the money that is raised go?

Sponsors have two options for the money raised: 1) Donate it to a nonprofit on our platform or 2) Donate it to a cause of their choice.

+What are some ways user can earn tokens?

Users can earn tokens by following different nonprofits and brands, by watching the ad campaigns, by logging in, through purchases, through interaction via comments on posts, by reading posted articles, and connecting with other users.

+What is a cause based video campaign?

A cause based video campaign is a campaign aimed at educating and shedding light on a particular cause.
