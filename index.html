About Our App

eCharityShop is a charitable social network and marketplace. Our goal is to increase the corporate social responsibility footprint within the giving community through partnerships and fundraising.

Cause Based Platform

We offer a cause based platform that allows brands and nonprofit organizations to share their political, social, and enviromental values with our audience through video.

Impression based Advertising

We in turn monetize the content and share a portion of the advertising revenue with the nonprofits every time a video is viewed.

Tokens as Rewards

Our network is powered by a micro-economy which uses tokens as rewards for watching cause based videos.

Purchase Pledges

When subscribers/users accumulate enough tokens they can use their tokens to purchase pledges donated by brand sponsors.

Purchase Ad Space

We partner with corporate sponsors interested in putting their social impact dollars to work by selling them advertising space on our platform in exchange for pledges in the form of in kind donations.

Introduce your Product

Once brands have fulfilled their donation requirments they can then introduce their product into the marketplace.

How Our App Works

Create

The network requires brands and nonprofits to create their own cause based video campaigns.

Earn Rewards

We use tokens as rewards for watching cause based videos. Accumulate tokens to purchase pledges donated by brand sponsors.

Monetize

Monetize through online advertising. We in turn share a portion of the proceeds with the organization.

Brand Partnership

We offer advertising space in exchange for pledges in the form of kind donations. Fulfill donation requirementsand introduce your product into the marketplace.

Our Sponsorship Packages

More than ever brands are using their influence to stand in solidarity with the causes they support, our platform represents an opportunity for corporations to use their branding power for social good through impactful ad campaigns. We offer brands a first hand look at how their ads and donations play a role in social and economic change.
