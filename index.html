About Our App

eCharityShop is a charitable social network and marketplace. Our goal is to increase the corporate social responsibility footprint within the giving community through partnerships and fundraising.

Cause Based Platform

We offer a cause based platform that allows brands and nonprofit organizations to share their political, social, and enviromental values with our audience through video.

Impression based Advertising

We in turn monetize the content and share a portion of the advertising revenue with the nonprofits every time a video is viewed.

Tokens as Rewards

Our network is powered by a micro-economy which uses tokens as rewards for watching cause based videos.

Purchase Pledges

When subscribers/users accumulate enough tokens they can use their tokens to purchase pledges donated by brand sponsors.

Purchase Ad Space

We partner with corporate sponsors interested in putting their social impact dollars to work by selling them advertising space on our platform in exchange for pledges in the form of in kind donations.

Introduce your Product

How Our App Works

Watch for Good is a fundraising social network and marketplace. What separates us from other crowdfunding platforms is our hybrid network that allows users to contribute financially by simply watching videos that include advertising. The network requires brands and nonprofits to create their own cause based video campaigns which are monetized through online advertising. We in turn share a portion of the proceeds with the nonprofit organizations

Our network is powered by a micro economy which uses tokens as rewards for watching cause based videos. When users accumulate enough tokens, they can then use their tokens to purchase pledges donated by brand sponsors. We partner with corporate sponsors interested in putting their social impact dollars to work by selling them advertising space and an e-commerce marketplace in exchange for pledges in the form of in-kind donations. Once brands have fulfilled their donations requirements they can introduce their product into the marketplace.
